Questions

Frequently Asked

Which AI platforms are supported?
Midjourney, DALL·E 3, Stable Diffusion, Adobe Firefly, and Ideogram. Each platform gets a specially formatted prompt with platform-specific syntax and parameters.
What is a negative prompt?
A negative prompt tells the AI what to avoid | things like blurry images, extra limbs, or unwanted styles. Toggle it on to get one automatically generated with your prompt.
Is this tool free?
Yes, completely free. No sign-up, no usage limits, no watermarks. Just describe your idea and generate.
Can I generate multiple variations?
Yes! Choose 1, 2, or 3 variations. Each variation takes a different creative angle of your idea | great for exploring different interpretations before committing to one.
