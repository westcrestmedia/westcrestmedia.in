Questions

Frequently Asked

Which video AI platforms are supported?
OpenAI Sora, Runway Gen-3, Pika Labs, Kling AI, Luma Dream Machine, and Stable Video Diffusion. Each gets platform-specific syntax and phrasing optimised for that tool.
What is a negative prompt for video?
It tells the AI what to avoid | things like camera shake, blurry footage, unrealistic motion, or unwanted visual elements. Toggle it on to include one automatically.
Is this tool completely free?
Yes, 100% free. No account needed, no usage limits, no watermarks. Describe your scene and generate instantly.
What do camera specs and director references add?
Camera specs add technical lens and movement details that push AI tools toward cinematic quality. Director references help anchor the visual language to a recognisable filmmaking style.
