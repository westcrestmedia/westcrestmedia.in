Questions

Frequently Asked

Is my image uploaded to any server?
No. The compression happens entirely inside your browser using JavaScript. Your image never leaves your device and is never sent to any server.
How much can I compress my images?
Depending on the image and quality setting, you can reduce file size by 50-80% while maintaining good visual quality.
Which formats are supported?
JPG, JPEG, PNG, and WEBP. PNG compression works best for photos; for logos/art with transparency, we convert to high-quality JPG.
Can I compress multiple images at once?
Yes! Batch compress up to 20 images at once. After compression, download them individually or as a single ZIP file.
