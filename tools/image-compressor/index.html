Questions

Frequently Asked

Which image formats are supported?
All of them! JPG, PNG, WEBP, GIF, BMP, TIFF, AVIF, HEIC, SVG, RAW (CR2, NEF, ARW), PSD, JP2, ICO, and more. The tool never rejects any format.
Can I convert format while compressing?
Yes! Select any output format from the dropdown. Your image will be compressed AND converted to that format.
Is my image uploaded?
Never. Everything happens locally in your browser. Your images stay private on your device.
Can I batch compress different formats?
Absolutely! Mix any formats — upload JPG, PNG, RAW, HEIC all together. They all get compressed to your chosen output format.
