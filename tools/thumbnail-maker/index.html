Questions

Frequently Asked

Is the thumbnail maker free to use?
Yes | completely free, no account required. Everything runs in your browser with no server upload.
What image formats can I export?
You can export as PNG (lossless) or JPG (smaller file size). Both export at the full canvas resolution you set.
What size should a YouTube thumbnail be?
YouTube recommends 1280×720 px (HD) or 1920×1080 px (Full HD). Both presets are available in the platform bar above.
Are my images uploaded to a server?
No | images never leave your device. All editing happens locally in your browser using the Canvas API.
