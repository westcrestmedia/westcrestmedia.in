Questions

Frequently Asked

Is Thumbnail Maker Pro free to use?
Yes — completely free. No sign-up, no watermarks, no limits. Design and export as many thumbnails as you need.
Does it work on mobile?
Yes. The editor is responsive and includes a mobile tool strip for quick access to text, colors, and effects on smaller screens.
What formats can I export?
You can export as PNG (best quality), JPG (smaller file), or WebP (optimized for web). All exports happen directly in your browser — nothing is uploaded.
Can I use my own images?
Absolutely. Upload PNG, JPG, WEBP, or SVG images from your device and layer them freely on the canvas with full position and opacity control.
