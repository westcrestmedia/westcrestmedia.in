Recent Palettes

Complementary

Two colors opposite on the color wheel. High contrast, vibrant — great for CTAs and highlights.

Triadic

Three evenly spaced colors. Balanced yet colorful — works well for diverse, energetic designs.

Analogous

Colors next to each other on the wheel. Harmonious and calm — perfect for natural, serene designs.

Monochromatic

Variations of a single hue. Sophisticated and cohesive — elegant for luxury and minimal aesthetics.

Split Complementary

A color plus two adjacent to its complement. Less tension than complementary, more variety.

Tetradic

Four colors forming a rectangle on the wheel. Rich palette — needs careful balance to not overwhelm.
