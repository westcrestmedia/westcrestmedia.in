Questions

Frequently Asked

Are these fonts free to use?
Yes — all suggested fonts are from Google Fonts and are completely free for personal and commercial use, with no licensing fees.
How do I use the CSS import in my project?
Click "Copy CSS" under any pairing. Paste the @import line into the top of your CSS file, then use the CSS variables (--font-heading, --font-body) throughout your stylesheet.
Can I preview with my own text?
Yes! After generating pairings, use the Custom Preview Text fields to type your own heading and body copy. The preview updates instantly.
Why does the AI sometimes suggest the same font twice?
Some fonts like Inter or Playfair Display work well at different weights for both headings and body. The AI may pair the same font family at different weights — this is a valid and popular typographic technique.
