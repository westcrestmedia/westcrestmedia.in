Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Are my images uploaded to any server?
No. All conversion happens entirely in your browser using the Canvas API. Your images never leave your device.
Is there a file size or count limit?
No limits at all. Convert as many images as you want, any size. Processing speed depends on your device's performance.
What formats are supported?
Input: JPG, PNG, WEBP, AVIF, BMP, TIFF, GIF, SVG, ICO, HEIC, WBMP. Output: JPG, PNG, WEBP, AVIF, BMP, GIF, TIFF, ICO, SVG, WBMP, and PDF.
Will the quality be affected?
You can control output quality using the quality slider (10–100%). For lossless formats like PNG, full quality is always preserved.
Does it work on mobile?
Yes, fully optimized for mobile browsers. Upload from your camera roll or files app and convert on the go.
