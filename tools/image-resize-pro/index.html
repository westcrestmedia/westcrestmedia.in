FAQ

Common questions

Is my image uploaded anywhere?
Never. All processing happens entirely in your browser using the Canvas API. Your images never leave your device.
Can I resize multiple images at once?
Yes! Upload up to 20 images and they all get resized with the same settings. Use "Download ZIP" to grab everything at once.
Will resizing reduce quality?
Downscaling is always lossless in terms of visual quality at the target size. Use the Quality slider (default 92%) for JPEG/WEBP output. For lossless output, choose PNG format.
What does "Fit Within Box" mean?
The image is scaled so it fits entirely within the target width and height, preserving aspect ratio. No cropping occurs. Empty space is filled with the background colour you choose.
What is the maximum image size?
Limited by your browser's canvas memory — typically up to 32 megapixels (e.g. 8000×4000px). Very large images may be slower on older devices.
Can I resize to a specific social media size?
Absolutely — use the Presets panel. Choose from 50+ ready-made sizes for Instagram, Twitter/X, Facebook, LinkedIn, YouTube, TikTok, Pinterest, and more.
