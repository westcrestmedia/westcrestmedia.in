Questions

Frequently Asked

Is my image uploaded to any server?
No. The AI model runs entirely inside your browser using WebAssembly. Your image never leaves your device and is never sent to any server.
How many images can I process at once?
Up to 20 images in a single batch. Images are processed one by one in sequence to avoid crashing your browser. After the first image loads the AI model, subsequent ones are much faster.
Why does the first use take longer?
The AI model (~50MB) is downloaded once and cached in your browser. All subsequent uses are instant — no re-download needed.
Can I add a real photo as background?
Yes — use the Photo Background panel to search Pexels and apply any photo as a background. You can also blur it for a professional depth-of-field effect.
